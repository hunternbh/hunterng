About Hunter

Hunter Ng graduated from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2021 with double first class honours degrees in Accountancy and Business Analytics. He has worked in Tokyo under Fujitsu's cloud computing department, worked in Singapore's Central Bank under the fintech division, and has run a floral boutique school in Singapore.

Hunter is currently a PhD candidate in Accountancy at Baruch College, City University of New York. His research interests lie in Generative AI, Cybersecurity and Accounting, Firm SEC Textual Disclosures and Environmental-Social-Governance ESG issues. Hunter is also experienced in web design, full-stack development and writing. He published his first novel - "As Coarse As Emporium" in 2021 under Jettison Books Pte. Ltd.

Hunter has speaking proficiency in English, Mandarin and Hindi. In his free time, he enjoys meditation, going to the gym, and appreciating movies.
