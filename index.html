About Hunter

Hunter graduated with two 1st Class Honours in Accountancy (Bachelors in Accountancy "BA") and Business Analytics (Bachelors in Business Administration "BBA") from Nanyang Technological University ("NTU"), Singapore. He holds a Graduate Certificate in Technopreneurship and Innovation ("GCTI") from NTU as well. He has a MBA from Baruch College and is currently a 3rd-year PhD candidate in Accountancy at Baruch College, City University of New York. His research energies lie in Generative AI, Cybersecurity and Accounting, Firm SEC Textual Disclosures and Environmental-Social-Governance ESG issues.

Hunter Ng has worked in Fujitsu's cloud computing department, Tokyo Japan, Singapore's Central Bank's (Monetary Authority of Singapore "MAS") fintech division on cryptocurrency, KPMG Singapore's Digital Trust Department. He set up his own floral boutique school in Singapore and has also published a Singaporean English novel that was previously listed for borrowing in National Library Board, Singapore. He has also sat on the executive-committee of a private shopping mall in Singapore together with Lawrence Leow from Crescendas. In his free time, he does full-stack software development and graphics design.
