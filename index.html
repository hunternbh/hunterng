About Hunter

Hunter Ng has worked in Fujitsu's cloud computing department, Tokyo Japan, Singapore's Central Bank's (Monetary Authority of Singapore) fintech division on cryptocurrency, and ran his own floral boutique school in Singapore.

Hunter is currently a PhD candidate in Accountancy at Baruch College, City University of New York. His research interests lie in Generative AI, Cybersecurity and Accounting, Firm SEC Textual Disclosures and Environmental-Social-Governance ESG issues.
